I’m currently doing my MSc in Sound and Music Computing at Universitat Pompeu Fabra within the Music Technology Group. I’m interested in Computational Music, Human-Centered AI, Music Information Retrieval, Music Structure, and Improvisation.

At BetterCart Analytics, I work as a Machine Learning Engineer on the ML/Data platform where I lead Machine Learning features and Information Retrieval systems that includes over 60,000,000 new records per week across 6,000+ stores and 100+ retailers.

I’m also a jazz trombonist playing in a variety of groups/settings that centers around improvisation. If interested, check out some music I’m -very slowly- writing on my soundcloud, the artists that influence me on my spotify profile, or come say “hey” at any upcoming shows I’m playing with some very talented and inspiring friends.

This site is currently a work in progress…Feel free to check out my twitter in the meantime. I like to tweet about music, computer science, sports, and other things.
